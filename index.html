I lead the Frontier Red Team at Anthropic. Our mission is to discover the most advanced (and possibly risky) capabilities of AGI, as far ahead as possible. We try to make our models do magic.

Before that, I was the Prime Minister's Special Adviser on science and technology in the UK. The basic idea was to build a country for the 22nd century -- change the structures of the state to cause new industrial revolutions. We made AI a national priority, created ARIA, allocated billions to science, made the Prime Minister's office a startup, and worked to prevent the largest national security risks. I learned a lot about the importance of extreme talent, the 'system', and the state of the world. I also helped build a moonshot at Google X and did a PhD in machine learning at Oxford.
